# 12 Invention of printing - 印刷术的发明

## 📖 原文精读

**A Chinese named Bi Sheng invented movable type print around 1,000 years ago.**  
一位名叫毕昇的中国人在大约1000年前发明了活字印刷术。

**The movable components are cubes made of baked clay.**  
可移动的组件是由烧制粘土制成的立方体。

**Characters were carved on each piece of clay, which looked like the seals widely used in China.**  
每个粘土块上都刻有字符，看起来像中国广泛使用的印章。

**The movable components could be arranged freely to form different vocabularies and sentences.**  
可移动组件可以自由排列，组成不同的词汇和句子。

**They were fixed on an iron plate with frames in certain sequence.**  
它们按照特定顺序被固定在带框架的铁板上。

**Then ink was painted and a piece of paper was laid down.**  
然后涂上墨水，铺上一张纸。

**A printed sheet was created this way.**  
通过这种方式创建了一张印刷页。

**This technique improved the efficiency of printing and transformed traditional transcription into modern printing.**  
这项技术提高了印刷效率，将传统抄写转变为现代印刷。

**Scripts and paintings spread widely in the world with the help of yìn shuā.**  
文字和绘画在印刷术的帮助下在世界范围内广泛传播。

## 📝 语法要点

### 1. 被动语态的使用
文章大量使用被动语态描述发明过程：
- "Characters **were carved** on each piece of clay"
- "They **were fixed** on an iron plate"
- "Then ink **was painted** and a piece of paper **was laid** down"

**语法规则**：被动语态 = be + 过去分词  
**用法场景**：强调动作对象而非执行者，常用于说明文、科技文献

### 2. 一般过去时描述历史事件
- "Bi Sheng **invented** movable type print"
- "This technique **improved** the efficiency"

**语法规则**：描述过去发生的、已完成的动作

### 3. 介词搭配
- **with frames** - 用框架（工具）
- **in certain sequence** - 按特定顺序
- **with the help of** - 在...的帮助下

```
  ↓

  ↓
制作工艺：carve characters → arrange freely → fix on iron plate
  ↓
印刷过程：paint ink → lay paper → create printed sheet
  ↓
历史意义：improved efficiency + transformed transcription + spread widely
```

## 🎯 关键理解点

### 1. 毕昇发明的核心理念
**"movable"** - 可移动性是最关键的创新点
- 之前的印刷：整块木板雕刻 → 无法重复使用
- 毕昇的发明：单个字模可自由组合 → 可重复使用

### 2. 技术细节
```
clay cubes → carve characters → arrange → fix on plate → ink → paper → print
(粘土块)   (刻字符)        (排列)  (固定铁板)  (涂墨) (铺纸) (印刷)
```

### 3. 历史影响
- **效率提升**：从抄写到印刷
- **传播加速**：scripts and paintings spread widely
- **文明推动**：为知识传播和文明发展奠定基础

## 🌏 文化背景

**毕昇 (Bi Sheng)** - 北宋时期 (1041-1048年) 发明家  
**活字印刷术** - 中国四大发明之一  
**传播路径**：中国 → 朝鲜 → 欧洲 (古腾堡，1450年左右)

## ✍️ 仿写练习

**题目**：描述一项发明的过程

**模板**：
```
[Inventor] invented [invention] around [time]. 
The [components] are made of [material]. 
[Parts] were [action] on each [component]. 
They could be [action] to [purpose]. 
They were fixed on [tool] in certain sequence. 
Then [action] was taken and [result] was created. 
This technique improved [aspect] and transformed [field].
```

**示例**（描述造纸术）：
```
Cai Lun invented paper around 2,000 years ago. 
The raw materials are tree bark, hemp, and rags. 
Fibers were extracted from each material. 
They could be mixed to form different textures. 
They were spread on a flat surface in certain thickness. 
Then pressure was applied and the paper was dried. 
This technique improved the efficiency of writing and transformed knowledge preservation.
```

## 📌 阅读技巧

### 1. 识别说明文结构
- **是什么** (invention, inventor, time)
- **怎么做** (materials, process, steps)
- **为什么重要** (impact, significance)

### 2. 关注连接词
- **then** - 表示时间顺序
- **with the help of** - 表示手段/工具
- **this way** - 指代前文描述的方法

### 3. 提取关键信息
**5W1H法**：
- Who: Bi Sheng
- When: around 1,000 years ago
- What: movable type print
- How: clay cubes → arrange → ink → paper
- Why: improve efficiency, spread knowledge

## 🔍 难点解析

### 1. "which looked like the seals widely used in China"
**结构分析**：
- main clause: "Characters were carved on each piece of clay"
- non-restrictive relative clause: "which looked like the seals"
- past participle phrase: "widely used in China" (修饰seals)

**翻译**：每个粘土块上刻有字符，看起来像中国广泛使用的印章

### 2. "with the help of yìn shuā"
**yìn shuā** = 印刷 (拼音)  
**句意**：在印刷术的帮助下
